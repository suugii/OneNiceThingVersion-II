Can doing one random act of kindness or one nice thing for someone really make a difference?

Have you every helped someone and wondered I wonder what effect it had on their lives.

Users of our site are encouraged to do nice things for others then encourage them to pass it on and keep it going. Then we offer a place where those people can share the nice things they've done with the people that started it.

This will provide a sequence of nice things that we can watch unfold and see the kind acts other perform and how long it stays going. We can also see how far it goes (geographically) from the original kind act. We've had users watch one act go all around the world.

You will receive an email each time a new nice thing is done that originated from you, or someone that signed up because of you. This will show what was done (because of you), and what each other person along the way did for one another.

Our goal is to make random acts of kindness go viral by encourage others to join in the fun of seeing how something so small can make a huge impact.
